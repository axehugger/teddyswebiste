The Puzzle Box

Himitsu-bako

In Japanese craftsmanship, a Himitsu-bako is a puzzle box—a container whose mechanism is hidden, requiring patience, insight, and an understanding of its architecture to open.

Every challenge in my career has been a puzzle box. From translating opaque AI systems into stories that build trust, to navigating the labyrinth of intellectual property clearance for billion-dollar brands, I've spent two decades finding the hidden mechanisms in impossible problems.
